## Termini di Ricerca

### Per trovare studi rilevanti
- "positive psychology interventions randomized trial"
- "metacognition learning transfer"
- "mindfulness RCT meta-analysis"
- "habit formation longitudinal study"
- "resilience training effectiveness"
- "self-directed learning adults"

### Filtri consigliati
- Ultimi 5 anni
- Peer-reviewed
- Systematic review o meta-analysis
- Full text available
